About Me

Hi, I'm Chosen.

I'm an IT student who builds weirdly specific things. I have a habit of turning random ideas into software—usually because I spend two hours searching for a tool that should exist, find nothing, and accidentally create it myself.

Most of my projects come from noticing small problems that don't seem to have the solution I'm looking for. Rather than waiting for someone else to build it, I try it myself.

I'm constantly learning, prompting AI, blaming it for every bug, and then quietly apologizing when I realize I was the one who forgot a semicolon.

I don't know my niche yet. But I know I love creating stuff no one has built before.
